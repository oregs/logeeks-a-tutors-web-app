FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

FAQs

Below are some pertinent questions you may want to ask us. These seem so far to be common/general questions which lingers in people's mind to ask us. So we thought it necessary to put the answers at your fingers' tip.

How much do we charge for tutorials?

We do not have a fixed price tag for private (or home-based) Tutorials. Tutorial charge is on a per-hour level and the amount varies from Tutor to Tutor. Our charges are determined by four(4) parameters: Tutor's experience, rating, academic qualification and the Company's vouch on the Tutor.

How can I hire a tutor?

To hire one of the Logeeks 'A'-Tutors, you must first have a Student account with us. You Login as a Student and then click 'Hire Tutor' to get started. Or you can sign up as a new Student.

How Trusted are the Tutors?

Just be calm and have a sense of optimum safety! This we say because, each Tutor's details have been verified under our Tutor-Verification Programme, and we only send verified Tutors to our Clients.

How can I join as Tutor?

You can write us a cover letter on paper or email it to us, attaching your C.V. to the letter. You can learn more about how to contact us, by clicking the 'Contact us' on our Web homepage. We will definitely reply you!

About Us

About Us


•  Who we are

The Logeeks 'A'-Tutors (Scientific) is an Educational Foundation, that provides Professional Tutors (and governesses) to Students (of Basic and Secondary Schools, O ‘level and A ‘level Candidates also), upon demand. We have our specialization in Science -for effectiveness- to transform Students into mind-blowing Scholars; even Scientists. We do not teach our Students just to pass Examinations, beyond this, we make Education lively and lovely to make our Students GET DEVELOPED!


•  What we do

Basically, we offer tutoring services to Students of Basic classes, Junior and Senior Secondary Students, either in the Tutorial center or private classes. Our services also extends to Candidates of WAEC, NECO, JAMB and A'level Exams.
